Medicinal Plants and Their Shona Names

Aloe Vera Papaya Garlic

Aloe Vera (Gavakava)

Used for burns, skin healing, digestive aid.

Extract gel and apply topically or consume small amounts of juice.

Papaya (Papaya)

Digestive aid, anti-parasitic, boosts immunity.

Eat ripe fruit, seeds for deworming, leaves in tea.

Garlic (Gariki)

Antimicrobial, lowers blood pressure, boosts immunity.

Crush cloves and consume raw or in meals, supplements possible.
